# 🥤 Coca-Cola Supply Chain Intelligence Dashboard

> **SCM 701 Final Project — Syracuse University, Whitman School of Management**  
> *What started as a supply chain report turned into a fully interactive data dashboard.*
---

## 📌 About This Project

For my **SCM 701 (Supply Chain Management)** final at Syracuse University's Whitman School of Management, I was assigned to write a report on Coca-Cola's supply chain. Standard assignment — pick a company, analyse the operations, submit.

But somewhere between reading about risk pooling, dynamic pricing, and their 250,000-vehicle global fleet, I got genuinely curious.

*How does a company move product to 30 million retail outlets across 200 markets and still hit 97.2% on-time delivery? How do you pool demand uncertainty across 6 regional hubs and unlock $380M in savings? How do you push through 9% net price realization in a year of FX headwinds?*

The report wasn't enough. So I built this.

## 📊 Dashboard Sections

| # | Topic | What's Covered |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 00 | **Overview** | End-to-end supply chain flow, KPIs, revenue by segment & geography, quarterly revenue 2022–2024 |
| 03 | **Risk Pooling** | Risk exposure scores, CV unpooled vs pooled by region, safety stock before/after pooling |
| 04 | **Dynamic Pricing** | SKU pricing board, price elasticity curve (ε = −1.4), seasonal price index |
| 05 | **Revenue Management** | Revenue bridge 2023→2024, channel mix, quarterly growth drivers (price vs volume vs mix) |
| 07 | **Transportation** | Route performance monitor, mode split, monthly freight cost index |
| 08 | **Warehousing** | Regional DC cards with utilization, cost structure, inventory turns by automation tier |

## 📈 Key Findings

```
📦 Risk Pooling
   Demand CV:  0.31 (unpooled)  →  0.12 (pooled)   ↓ 61%
   Safety Stock Reduction:  −18%
   Annual Savings:  $380M

💰 Dynamic Pricing
   Price Elasticity (ε):  −1.4  (core sparkling SKUs)
   Net Price Realization 2024:  +9%
   Organic Revenue Growth Q4 2024:  +14%

🏭 Revenue Management
   Gross Profit Margin:  59.7%   ↑ 1.2pp YoY
   Operating Margin:  27.4%
   Revenue per Unit Case:  $6.82  (↑ from $6.41)

🚛 Transportation
   Fleet:  ~250,000 vehicles globally
   AI-Routed:  94% of all routes
   On-time Delivery:  97.2%   ↑ 0.8pp
   EV Fleet Share:  12%   ↑ 4pp YoY

🏢 Warehousing
   Total Space:  42M m²
   Avg Utilization:  84%
   Pick Accuracy:  99.7%
   Cross-dock Turns:  28.6×/year  vs  9.8× manual
```
